core services

Core services provided by the platform include:

Automated Claims Processing: It acts as a single-window system that links hospitals to all major insurance companies and TPAs. This digitizes pre-authorizations and manages the entire claims lifecycle from submission to final settlement.

Instant Patient Financing: To reduce out-of-pocket stress for patients, the platform facilitates embedded financing options at the time of discharge. It offers flexible EMIs and quick loan approvals to ease cash-flow burdens.Claim Tracking & Grievance Handling: It manages end-to-end claim tracking with real-time status updates, reducing the administrative load and paperwork on hospital staff. It includes dedicated support to handle claim rejections and grievances.
